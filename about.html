About

Tyler Zemantic

Tyler Zemantic is currently a senior at the University of Connecticut. He is majoring in Digital Media and Design where his concentration is in digital business strategies. His focus is in digital and social media marketing. Other strong skills of his are in web and graphic design/development.

Tyler Zemantic has strong skills in communication and collaboration. Working in digital media, it is essential for him to be a team player, and to have consistent, clear communication. In the realm of digital marketing, social media is Tyler's biggest strength. By studying trends and analytics, Tyler develops marketing strategies for businesses via their social channels.

On his free time Tyler enjoys traveling and being outdoors. Skateboarding and snowboarding have always been a passion of his, and a lot of his work is inspired from these interests.
