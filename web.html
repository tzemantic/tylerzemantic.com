Ziga Mineral

David Ziga is a good friend of mine and he came to me with a problem: his website wasn't looking good at all, and was very outdated to say the least. He needed someone to totally redesign and develope a website that would fit his brand. Ziga mineral is a company that connects people with the gems they are looking for. The purpose of this website is to act as a portfolio, showcasing some of the best finds ziga mineral has. I developed this in sublime text using html, css, javascript, and bootstrap as a framework.
