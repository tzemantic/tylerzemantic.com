Web

Ziga Mineral

David Ziga is a good friend of mine and he came to me with a problem: his website wasn't looking good at all and was very outdated to say the least. He needed someone to totally redesign and develop a website that would fit his brand. Ziga Mineral is a company that connects people with the gems they are looking for. The purpose of this website is to act as a portfolio, showcasing some of the best finds Ziga Mineral has. I developed this in sublime text using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and bootstrap as a framework.

Jimmies of Savin Rock

Jimmies of Savin rock is a family-owned restaurant on the shore of New Haven, Connecticut. My good friend Jamie Gagliardi set me and a partner up with the job of re-designing and developing their website. Creating this website was a team effort. I was initially responsible for getting most of the content: photos, videos, logos etc. My parnter set up the initial HTML and CSS. I then refined and optimized the final product.

Casa Azzura

This was a website I made for fun. My good friend works at a barbershop, which inspired me to develop this concept piece. I used HTML and CSS while using bootstrap as a framework to develop this website. While this contains content for a barbershop, this layout could be interchanged for various businesses.

UConn SMACC

(Social Media Analytics Command Center)

This is a website that I am currently developing for the University of Connecticut's Social Media Analytics Command Center, part of the Digital Media and Design program. After seeing some of my previous work, my advisor/instructor asked me if I was interested in developing the website for the SMACC center. I am working on this with one other student in the DMD program. This website is to showcase what the SMACC center does with social media analytics and content strategy. This is also my main area of study within the DMD program.
